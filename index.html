Real Linux

Nebula Terminal x86 / WASM

Not a simulation — an actual x86 Linux machine emulated in the browser with v86, piped through xterm.js over the emulated serial port. It boots a genuine kernel and drops you at a real shell. (Fetches the engine and kernel on load.)
